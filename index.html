Services

There are 3 variations of our service, which can be tailored even further to your exact needs.

Run a Project / One-and-Done

Have a fixed bunch of data you need to have meticulously annotated? We offer flexible one-and-done type of agreements.

Run a Pipeline / Subscription

Have a continuous stream of data you need to have annotated, always in the same way? A subscription to our service might offer you the best bang for your buck.

Run a Team / Hire Fixed Team

Your annotation protocols may vary but you prefer a team of annotators to work with? We can assign a fixed annotator team to your project managers.
